About Me

Hello! Welcome to my Portfolio! I am Juvelyn Joy, a passionate and innovative 2nd year Multimedia Arts student studying at Philippine Christian University-Dasmariñas. I am committed to using visual storytelling to bring out artistic visions. I have a great appreciation for aesthetics and a sharp eye for details, and I specialize in turning ideas into artworks that capture the viewer's attention.
